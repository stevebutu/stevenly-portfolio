This is the first website project I had ever made. While this time and setting where I was in life, I wanted to express my love for my love for old cars and the mixture of new cars. How old cars can progress from something small and compact with a hint of vintage be transformed into a sports machine! The fact that we tend to look down on these cars due to time has mind boggled me, so this piece is for the remeberance of old cars! The program I used to code this website was from Sublime.
