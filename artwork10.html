For this piece, I worked together with my peers Zachary and Eddie. The theme was where we wanted to present a nature feeling to the environment with the mixture of mythology to tie in with the environment. It has a good versus evil story; the goblin heads were the symbol of evil while the unicorn and fluffy dog-bunnies were a symbol of good force protecting the area. The piece was primarily made with Adobe Dimension.
