The concept was to give my friend, the model, an "Instagram" look for social media platforms. The trend of looking like a model or, more enough, having a look at what you want to look like on these platforms has been huge. My friend here wanted to show himself as a model, someone who wants to become somewhere in the modeling industry in the future. The programs I used for this specific photoshoot were made with Lightroom Classic. The rest of the album has been private due to my friend using it for his social media platform.
