Ones With No Voice is a call to action that I had made recently at home for my Art 13 class. This 3D Font Project aims to bring recognition and voice to the Asian American community, who have been targeted with verbal harassment and physical assaults throughout the country. This marks my position and progression as an artist who is slowly changing my style as an artist. The materials used were a hot glue gun, paper, and cardboard.
